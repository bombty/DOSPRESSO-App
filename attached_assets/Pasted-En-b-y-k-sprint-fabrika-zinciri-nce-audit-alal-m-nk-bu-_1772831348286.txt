### BÖLÜM A: Mevcut Fabrika Tablo Yapısı

```sql
-- 1. Fabrika ile ilgili TÜM tablolar ve kayıt sayıları
SELECT t.tablename, s.n_live_tup as kayit_sayisi
FROM pg_stat_user_tables s
JOIN information_schema.tables t ON t.table_name = s.tablename
WHERE t.tablename ILIKE '%factory%' OR t.tablename ILIKE '%fabrika%'
OR t.tablename ILIKE '%production%' OR t.tablename ILIKE '%uretim%'
OR t.tablename ILIKE '%inventory%' OR t.tablename ILIKE '%stok%'
OR t.tablename ILIKE '%stock%' OR t.tablename ILIKE '%warehouse%'
OR t.tablename ILIKE '%batch%' OR t.tablename ILIKE '%shipment%'
OR t.tablename ILIKE '%sevkiyat%' OR t.tablename ILIKE '%raw_material%'
OR t.tablename ILIKE '%hammadde%' OR t.tablename ILIKE '%ingredient%'
OR t.tablename ILIKE '%kiosk%' OR t.tablename ILIKE '%station%'
ORDER BY s.n_live_tup DESC;

-- 2. Kritik tabloların kolon yapıları
-- factory_inventory
SELECT column_name, data_type, is_nullable FROM information_schema.columns 
WHERE table_name = 'factory_inventory' ORDER BY ordinal_position;

-- factory_production_batches
SELECT column_name, data_type, is_nullable FROM information_schema.columns 
WHERE table_name = 'factory_production_batches' ORDER BY ordinal_position;

-- inventory_movements
SELECT column_name, data_type, is_nullable FROM information_schema.columns 
WHERE table_name = 'inventory_movements' ORDER BY ordinal_position;

-- factory_products (veya products)
SELECT column_name, data_type, is_nullable FROM information_schema.columns 
WHERE table_name = 'factory_products' ORDER BY ordinal_position;

-- factory_raw_materials (veya raw_materials veya ingredients)
SELECT column_name, data_type, is_nullable FROM information_schema.columns 
WHERE table_name ILIKE '%raw_material%' OR table_name ILIKE '%ingredient%'
ORDER BY table_name, ordinal_position;

-- shipments / sevkiyat
SELECT column_name, data_type, is_nullable FROM information_schema.columns 
WHERE table_name ILIKE '%shipment%' OR table_name ILIKE '%sevkiyat%'
ORDER BY table_name, ordinal_position;

-- factory_quality_checks
SELECT column_name, data_type, is_nullable FROM information_schema.columns 
WHERE table_name ILIKE '%quality%' AND table_name ILIKE '%factory%'
ORDER BY ordinal_position;
```

Raporla:
- Kaç fabrika tablosu var? Her birinin kayıt sayısı
- Hangi tablolar boş (0 kayıt)?
- Stok tablosunda hangi kolonlar var? (miktar, birim, minimum stok, lokasyon)
- Batch tablosunda neler var? (üretim miktarı, reçete ref, durum, kalite onay)
- Sevkiyat tablosu var mı? Yapısı nasıl?
