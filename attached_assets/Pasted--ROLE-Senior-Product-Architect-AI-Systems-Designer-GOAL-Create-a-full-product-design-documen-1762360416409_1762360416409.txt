[ROLE]: Senior Product Architect & AI Systems Designer

[GOAL]:
Create a full product design document for the **DOSPRESSO Franchise Management WebApp**.
The platform must allow DOSPRESSO HQ to manage and monitor all franchise branches, tasks, personnel performance, equipment, training, and AI-assisted verification.
The entire **software, database fields, interface texts, and user-facing language must be in Turkish.**
The documentation and explanations can remain in English.

[CORE OBJECTIVES]:
1. Centralize branch operations under one management platform.
2. Verify photo-based tasks with AI image analysis.
3. Manage assignments, education, maintenance, and equipment tracking.
4. Provide automatic reminders, performance metrics, and summarized AI dashboards.
5. Include an **AI Knowledge Base (KB)** system that HQ can continuously update and train the AI with new machine/equipment info, recipes, procedures, and visual guides.
6. Maintain DOSPRESSO’s professional identity, standards, and visual design.

- **HQ Roles**
  - Muhasebe

[AI FEATURES]:
- Photo analysis → task verification, error detection, improvement suggestions.  
- Fault reporting → image + text analysis, automated solution guidance.  
- Education & exam evaluation → auto grading and personalized feedback.  
- Automatic reminders → every 10 minutes if checklist not completed.  
- Recipe & machine calibration assistant → step-by-step guidance.  
- KPI & performance scoring dashboards.  
- Summarized visual + textual AI reporting for managers.  
- **AI Knowledge Base (KB) & Admin CMS:**  
  HQ can upload SOPs, recipes, calibration settings, maintenance guides, and visuals to train the AI system.  
  The AI uses RAG (retrieval-augmented generation) to answer only from approved HQ content.  
  Content types: SOP, Recipe, Calibration, Troubleshooting, Safety Notice, Spare Parts Catalog, FAQ.

Languages: **All interfaces, field names, DB labels, UI components, and text outputs must be in Turkish.**

[AI KNOWLEDGE BASE (DETAIL)]:
- KB-Service microservice:  
  - Storage: S3 (media), Postgres (metadata), Vector DB (pgvector)  
  - Embedding: text + image captions  
  - Approval workflow: draft → review → approved → archived  
  - API endpoints (all responses in Turkish):  
    - POST /admin/kb/icerikler (create draft)  
    - POST /admin/kb/icerikler/{id}/onayla (approve & embed)  
    - GET /kb/ara?q&etiket&rol&sube_id  
    - POST /kb/cevapla {soru, rol, sube_id, kaynak:true}  
  - Role-based visibility (HQ / branch / specific role).  
  - Audit logs and version control.  
  - AI uses only **approved Turkish content** for responses.

[NOTIFICATION & REMINDER LOGIC]:
- Task not completed → reminder every 10 minutes.  
- New critical KB entry → push/email notification to relevant roles.  
- “Unread after 24h” → reminder; “Unread after 72h” → flagged as noncompliant.
