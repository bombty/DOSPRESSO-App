## Academy HQ Management Panel — Complete Overhaul

### Context
The /akademi-hq page is a 2300-line monolithic file with broken tabs (Modüller tab has no TabsTrigger), missing features (no webinar tab), and hardcoded data. This sprint refactors it into a clean, modular management panel with 7 tabs.

Read these skills first:
- .agents/skills/dospresso-architecture/SKILL.md
- .agents/skills/dospresso-quality-gate/SKILL.md

### CRITICAL RULES
- Turkish UI, dark mode compatible, no hardcoded colors, no emoji (Lucide icons)
- Use CompactKPIStrip for mobile KPI cards
- Use MobileFilterCollapse for filter sections
- Shadcn/ui components (Tabs, Card, Badge, Dialog, Switch, Select, Table)
- Do NOT touch server/vite.ts, vite.config.ts, package.json, drizzle.config.ts
- users.id is VARCHAR, soft delete pattern
