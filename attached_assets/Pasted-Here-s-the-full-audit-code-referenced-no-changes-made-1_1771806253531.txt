5) What the AI Does NOT Do (Guardrails)
No automatic writes:

The AI panel is read-only. It generates suggestions/signals but never creates tasks, updates scores, or modifies any user data.
The only write is appending audit rows to ai_agent_logs.
No LLM calls:

Zero external API calls. All logic is deterministic — threshold checks (score < 60, compositeScore < 50, trainingScore < 40), date comparisons (overdue onboarding), and rule matching (KPI signals).
No automatic task creation:

Actions displayed to employees are suggestions with title, reason, and estimatedMinutes. There is no "accept" or "auto-create" button.
Human approval documented:

docs/04-ai-surface-rules.md Pattern 2 (line 31-37): "AI can read and recommend freely. AI cannot write without a human in the loop."
The panel displays — the human decides whether to act.
No data modification from risk signals:

Supervisor sees risk signals but must manually navigate to team management to take action. No automated interventions.
